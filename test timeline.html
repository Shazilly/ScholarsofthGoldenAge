Detailed Chronological List

8th - 9th Century (Early Golden Age)
1. Jabir ibn Hayyan (c. 721 – c. 815) - Father of early chemistry, alchemy
2. Fatima al-Fihri (c. 800 – c. 880) - Founder of University of al-Qarawiyyin
3. Al-Khwarizmi (c. 780 – c. 850) - Mathematics, Algebra, Algorithms
4. Hunayn ibn Ishaq (c. 808 – 873) - Medicine, Translation
5. Al-Kindi (c. 801 – 873) - Philosophy, Cryptography, Medicine
6. Abbas ibn Firnas (c. 810 – 887) - Engineering, Aviation pioneer
7. Abu Bakr Al-Razi (c. 865 – c. 925) - Medicine, Chemistry, Philosophy
8. Al-Farabi (c. 872 – c. 950) - Philosophy, Political science, Logic
9. Yahya ibn Adi (c. 893 – 974) - Philosophy, Logic, Translation
10th Century
10. Sutayta Al-mahamali (10th century) - Mathematics, Algebra
11. Mariam al-Ijliya (10th century) - Astronomy, Instrument design
12. Abu al-Qasim al-Zahrawi (c. 936 – c. 1013) - Surgery, Medical instruments
10th - 11th Century (Peak Period)
13. Al-Biruni (c. 973 – c. 1050) - Astronomy, History, Anthropology
14. Ibn Sina (Avicenna) (c. 980 – 1037) - Medicine, Philosophy, Science
15. Alhazen (Ibn al-Haytham) (c. 965 – c. 1040) - Optics, Scientific method
11th - 14th Century (Later Period)
16. Omar Khayyam (c. 1048 – 1131) - Mathematics, Astronomy, Poetry
17. Ibn Rushd (1126 – 1198) - Philosophy, Law, Medicine
18. Ismail al-Jazari (c. 1136 – 1206) - Engineering, Robotics, Automation
19. Ibn al-Nafis (c. 1213 – 1288) - Medicine, Pulmonary circulation
20. Ibn Khaldun (1332 – 1406) - Historiography, Sociology, Economics
